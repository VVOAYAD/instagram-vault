# Aesthetic Lock — @alvvoayadcreates

Every image generated by the pipeline inherits this DNA. Nothing gets posted that doesn't match.

## Core DNA
Y2K chrome-core meets dreamcore meets cosmic romanticism. Painted, illustrated, textured — **never photorealistic**. Feels like a scan from a 2003 magazine that fell through a wormhole.

## Palette world
Chrome, liquid silver, deep navy, starfield black, pastel pink, sky blue with
clouds, pink and gold glitter, prismatic rainbow chromatic aberration, amber
and violet painterly skies, jewel tones with gold sparkle. All colors pulled
from the attached reference images — follow those, not any named list. Avoid
acid green and neon magenta gradients unless the refs clearly show them.

## Motifs (pick 1–2 per slide)
Chrome liquid metal · tribal chrome ornaments · metallic angel figures · 4-point sparkles · lens flares · prismatic light bursts · starfields · nebulae · celestial bodies · 90s cel-shaded anime hands · glossy nails · film grain · airbrush texture · rainbow chromatic aberration · subtle VHS lines

## What it is NOT
Not photorealistic. Not clean vector. Not minimalist flat. Not generic-AI "cosmic woman meditating." No modern clean UI typography.

## Typography (baked into art by Gemini)
- **Affirmations:** ALL CAPS, wide letter-spacing, chrome/gold fill, subtle glow
- **Philosophy lines:** italic serif (Cormorant-style), soft, romantic
- **Corner labels:** tiny monospace, manga-scan style ("a step closer to you")
- Text lives *inside* the art — same grain, same lighting. Never overlayed.

## Composition
4:5 vertical · 1080×1350 · central subject OR edge-to-edge texture · high contrast · luminous focal point (always something glowing) · grain on everything

## Themes (rotate per post)
consciousness · nervous system regulation · knowing thyself · self-worth · standing on business · growth · expansion · evolution · becoming

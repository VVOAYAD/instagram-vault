Your nervous system does not need another strategy.
It needs your presence.

Most of what controls you is not unhealed — it is unfelt. Unseen. Unwitnessed.

The moment you can sit with a sensation without needing to change it, fix it, escape it, or understand it — something ancient in the body relaxes.

Not because the threat is gone.
But because someone finally showed up.

Ash-Shuhūd is the oldest technology of transformation. Not watching from the mind. Witnessing from the body. From the one who was here before the story. Before the wound. Before the pattern.

You do not need to become someone new.
You need to return to the one who was always watching.

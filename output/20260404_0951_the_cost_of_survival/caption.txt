Your nervous system made a decision before you had language.

It chose the known over the felt.
The managed over the alive.
The controlled over the true.

And it worked. You survived.

But somewhere along the way, the thing that saved you became the thing that sealed you in.

Regulation without presence is just a higher-functioning freeze.
Comfort without depth is just numbness with better furniture.

The work is not to override the pattern.
It is to finally show your body that the emergency is over.

That it is allowed to choose something other than safe.
That life was always on the other side of the wall it built.
